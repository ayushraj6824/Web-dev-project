About Us

We help folks lose, heal, understand, and move their bodies - so they can be happier, healthier humans.

Our Mission

We're here to get as many people moving as possible. Our mission is to make movement accessible and enjoyable for all people, emphasizing that exercise should be associated with joy and not pain.
